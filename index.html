How to play

Each round names a real NYC spot. Tap the (label-free!) map where you think it is, then hit Confirm.

Rounds get harder: famous landmarks first, then museums & parks, then bars & restaurants. Later rounds give fewer hints and score tighter.

Land inside the target zone for the full 1000 points; points fall off with distance. 5 rounds, 5000 max.

Everyone gets the same 5 spots each day. Come back daily to build your 🔥 streak.
