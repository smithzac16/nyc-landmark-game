How to play

Each round names an NYC landmark. Tap the (label-free!) map where you think it is, then hit Confirm.

Within ~75 m earns the full 1000 points; points fall off the farther away you are. 5 rounds, 5000 max.

Everyone gets the same 5 landmarks each day. Come back daily to build your 🔥 streak.
